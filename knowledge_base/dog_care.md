# Dog Care Guidelines

## Daily Care Tasks
- **Feeding**: Adult dogs need 2 meals per day (morning and evening), 20–30 min each feeding window.
- **Water**: Refresh water bowl at least twice daily. Dehydration is a common issue.
- **Exercise/Walk**: Dogs need 30–60 minutes of exercise per day. Puppies and high-energy breeds (Labs, Border Collies) need 60–90 min.
- **Toilet breaks**: At minimum every 4–6 hours; puppies need every 1–2 hours.

## Weekly Care Tasks
- **Grooming/Brushing**: Short-hair breeds: 1x/week. Long-hair breeds: 3–4x/week.
- **Ear cleaning**: Check weekly, clean monthly or as needed (especially floppy-eared breeds).
- **Nail trim**: Every 2–4 weeks depending on activity level.
- **Bath**: Most dogs: once every 4–6 weeks unless muddy. Over-bathing strips natural oils.

## Monthly / Periodic Tasks
- **Flea/tick prevention**: Monthly topical or oral treatment recommended.
- **Heartworm prevention**: Monthly pill, year-round.
- **Dental brushing**: Ideally daily; at minimum 3x/week to prevent tartar buildup.

## Vet Schedule
- **Puppy** (0–1 yr): Vet visits every 3–4 weeks for vaccines.
- **Adult** (1–7 yr): Annual wellness exam + vaccines.
- **Senior** (7+ yr): Bi-annual exams; increased risk of arthritis, dental disease, organ issues.

## Scheduling Tips
- Morning walk + feeding: 7:00–8:00 AM (30–60 min combined)
- Evening walk + feeding: 5:30–6:30 PM
- Medication tasks: HIGH priority, same time daily for consistency
- Training sessions: 10–15 min, HIGH or MEDIUM priority, best in morning when alert
