# Cat Care Guidelines

## Daily Care Tasks
- **Feeding**: Most adult cats do well with 2 meals/day. Free-feeding dry food is acceptable for healthy-weight cats.
- **Water**: Cats often prefer running water; refresh daily. Consider a water fountain to encourage hydration.
- **Litter box**: Scoop at least once daily. Cats are fastidious and may avoid a dirty box.
- **Interactive play**: 10–15 minutes of interactive play (wand toy, laser) twice daily, especially for indoor cats.

## Weekly Care Tasks
- **Grooming**: Short-hair: brush 1x/week. Long-hair: daily brushing to prevent matting.
- **Litter box deep clean**: Full litter change every 1–2 weeks.
- **Nail trim**: Every 2–3 weeks. Indoor cats especially need regular trims.
- **Dental check**: Brush teeth 2–3x/week if possible; dental treats are a backup option.

## Monthly / Periodic Tasks
- **Flea/tick prevention**: Monthly topical treatment recommended, especially for cats with outdoor access.
- **Weight check**: Weigh monthly; obesity is common and leads to diabetes, joint issues.
- **Enrichment rotation**: Swap toys weekly to maintain mental stimulation.

## Vet Schedule
- **Kitten** (0–1 yr): Series of vaccines at 8, 12, 16 weeks; spay/neuter at ~6 months.
- **Adult** (1–10 yr): Annual wellness exam.
- **Senior** (10+ yr): Bi-annual exams; watch for hyperthyroidism, kidney disease, dental disease.

## Scheduling Tips
- Morning feeding: 7:00–7:30 AM (15 min)
- Play session: 7:30–7:45 AM and 6:00–6:15 PM
- Litter scoop: 8:00 AM daily (10 min), LOW priority but must be done
- Evening feeding: 6:15–6:30 PM
- Medication tasks: HIGH priority, administer with food to reduce stress
